Great Tasty Fried Chicken

Description:

Hooters' Traditional Wings are classic, crispy, deep-fried chicken wings, tossed in your choice of sauce. Known for their golden-brown crunch and juciy interior, these wings come with a variety of bold flavors ranging from mild to extra hot. They are served with a side of ranch or blue

Ingridents:

For the patties:

  • 1lb ground beef (80/20 for juciness)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic (optional)

For the Buns:

  • 4 burger buns (preferably potato or brioche)
  • 2 tbsp butter (softened, for toasting)

For the Toppings:

  • 4 slices American cheese (or Wisconsin cheddar)
  • 4-6 dill pickle slices
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 4 tomato slices
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions (optional)
