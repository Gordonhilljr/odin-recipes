Have you ever had better pasta??!

Description:

Olive Garden's Chicken and Broccoli Alfredo is a creamy, indulgent pasta dish featuring tender grilled chicken, fresh broccoli, and fettuccine noodles tossed in their signature Alfredo sauce. The dish is rich, savory, and perfectly balance with the combination of garlic, Parmesan, and cream.

Ingridents:

Protein & Vegetables:

  • Grilled chicken breast
  • Fresh broccoli florets

Pasta & Sauce:

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter
  • Garlic(minced)
  • Parmesan cheese (freshly grated)
  • Romano cheese (optional)
  • Milk (or half-and-half)
  • Salt & black pepper
  • Nutmeg (optional, for depth of flavor)

Garnish:

  • Parsley (chopped, optional)
  • Extra Parmesan cheese
