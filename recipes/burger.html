Good Burgers

Description:

A Culver’s ButterBurger-inspired cheeseburger features a juicy, seared beef patty cooked with a crispy outer crust, topped with real Wisconsin cheddar or American cheese, and served on a lightly buttered and toasted bun. To make it at home, press thin beef patties on a hot griddle for a caramelized crust, season with salt and pepper, and top with cheese. Serve on a toasted, buttered bun with optional toppings like pickles, lettuce, tomato, and Culver’s signature mayo-mustard-ketchup blend. Enjoy the classic Midwestern fast-food favorite!

Ingridents:

For the patties:

  • 1lb ground beef (80/20 for juciness)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic (optional)

For the Buns:

  • 4 burger buns (preferably potato or brioche)
  • 2 tbsp butter (softened, for toasting)

For the Toppings:

  • 4 slices American cheese (or Wisconsin cheddar)
  • 4-6 dill pickle slices
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 4 tomato slices
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions (optional)
